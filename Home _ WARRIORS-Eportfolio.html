    ABOUT

     The  study aims to develop and implement a hybrid energy harvesting system using solar and piezoelectric crystal with IoT which will be able to harvest energy from sunlight and rainfall with the help of piezoelectric crystals and solar cells with an added inclusion of a monitoring system through the web application. The raw data being processed will be used to prove efficiency and predicting of the power output of the system.
